\section{Visual Task Hierarchy}
Sensation $\to$ Processing $\to$ Perception $\to$ Cognition.

(1) Acquisition. RGB/depth cameras, LiDAR $\to$ images, video, point clouds, meshes.
(2) Low-Level. Processing and feature extraction: denoising, deblurring, watermark removal, edges/corners.
(3) Mid-Level. Real-world inference: 3D reconstruction, panorama stitching.
(4) High-Level. Semantic representation and interpretation: scene understanding, AR.
