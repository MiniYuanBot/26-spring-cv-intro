\section{Instance Segmentation}
\subsection{Mask R-CNN Pipeline}
\subsubsection*{Three-Head Architecture}
Based on Faster R-CNN with added mask head. Pipeline: Backbone $\to$ RPN $\to$ RoIAlign $\to$ three parallel heads: cls, reg, mask.
Training: mask head supervised only on positive RoIs.

\subsubsection*{RoIAlign}
Avoids quantization of RoI Pool. Steps: (1) keep floating-point proposal locations; (2) divide into bins; (3) sample 4 points per bin with bilinear interpolation; (4) pool (max/avg). Significant AP gain, especially at high IoU thresholds.

\subsection{Mask Head Design}
\subsubsection*{Output Strategy}
Use per-pixel Sigmoid (independent binary classification) instead of Softmax across classes. Avoids class competition since cls head already decides category; mask head focuses purely on shape.

\subsubsection*{Channel Strategy}
Class-specific: output $K$ channels, one binary mask per class. Parameters scale with $K$.
Class-agnostic: single shared mask channel. Mask R-CNN uses class-specific $K$ channels but only the channel predicted by cls head is used for loss and inference.

\subsubsection*{Loss}
Per-pixel binary cross-entropy over $m\times m$ mask (e.g., $28\times28$):
\[
\mathcal{L}_{mask}=-\frac{1}{m^2}\sum_{u=1}^m\sum_{v=1}^m\bigl[y_{uv}\log\hat y_{uv}+(1-y_{uv})\log(1-\hat y_{uv})\bigr]
\]
Only the GT class channel contributes gradients; other channels ignored.
