\section{Instance Segmentation}
\subsection{Mask R-CNN Pipeline}
\subsubsection*{Architecture}
\textbf{Top-down} Architecture. Based on Faster R-CNN with added mask head. Three parallel heads: cls, reg, mask.
Training: mask head supervised only on positive RoIs.

\subsubsection*{RoIAlign}
Avoid quantization of RoI Pool.
(1) keep float proposal locations;
(2) divide into bins;
(3) sample 4 points per bin with bilinear interpolation;
(4) pool (max/avg).
Significant AP gain, especially at high IoU thresholds.

\subsection{Mask Head Design}
\subsubsection*{Output Strategy}
Use per-pixel Sigmoid (independent binary classification) instead of Softmax across classes.
Avoids class competition since cls head already decides category; mask head focuses purely on shape.

\subsubsection*{Channel Strategy}
Class-specific: $K$ binary mask channels, params $\propto K$. Class-agnostic: single shared mask.
Mask R-CNN: $K$ channels but only the cls-selected one is used for loss and inference.

\subsubsection*{Loss}
Per-pixel binary cross-entropy over $m\times m$ mask (e.g., $28\times28$):
\[
\mathcal{L}_{mask}=-\frac{1}{m^2}\sum_{u=1}^m\sum_{v=1}^m\bigl[y_{uv}\log\hat y_{uv}+(1-y_{uv})\log(1-\hat y_{uv})\bigr]
\]
Only the GT class channel contributes gradients; other channels ignored.
